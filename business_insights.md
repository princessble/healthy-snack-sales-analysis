# Business Insights

## 1. Best-Selling Product
The product with the highest quantity sold was Peanut Snack, with 36 units sold. This shows that customers are responding well to this product.

## 2. Highest Revenue Product
The product with the highest revenue was Kale Chips, generating €108.00. This product should be prioritised in marketing and production.

## 3. Most Profitable Product
The product with the highest profit was Kale Chips, generating €60.00 profit. This is important because high revenue does not always mean high profit.

## 4. Best Location
The location with the highest revenue was Dublin, generating €198.10. This location could be a strong target for future promotions.

## 5. Customer Type Insight
The customer type that generated the most revenue was Individual customers, with €192.60. This can help the business focus its sales strategy.

## 6. Business Recommendations
Based on the analysis, the business should:
- Continue producing Peanut Snack because it had the highest quantity sold.
- Prioritise Kale Chips because it generated the highest revenue and profit.
- Focus more marketing efforts in Dublin because it was the strongest sales location.
- Create offers for individual customers because they generated the highest revenue.
- Track monthly sales regularly to understand customer demand and seasonal trends.
